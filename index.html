LearnBosnia(n)

...A place where learning begins...

Hello! Welcome to my webpage! I am Asja and I decided to make a website in which I will present Bosnian language. I want to help young children who live in foreign countries to learn or improve their mother tongue (Bosnian). In this webpage you can find differenent videos, examples, texts and exercises. You can also connect with us on different social platforms such as Instagram and Facebook. I hope my webpage is useful to you and I hope you enjoy!

Dobar dan i dobrodošli na moju web stranicu. Ja sam Asja i odlucila sam da napravim stranicu na kojoj cu predstavity Bosanski jezik.
